Our Mission

At Shamba AI, we're on a mission to transform agriculture in Africa through the power of artificial intelligence and data analytics.

Our platform combines satellite imagery, weather data, soil analysis, and farming best practices to provide actionable insights that help farmers increase yields, reduce waste, and practice sustainable agriculture.

Founded by a team of AI specialists and agricultural experts, Shamba AI is committed to addressing the unique challenges faced by African farmers and contributing to food security across the continent.
